Going to the gym is hard. We've all been there and felt the same way. Even more if we are students busy with coursework. HealthyU, which stands for "Healthy University", aims to promote healthy living to part time and full time students attending university. HealthyU aims to not transform individuals into the mold of beauty standards of today and encourages body positivity by providing students with a with a low-intensity, simple plan in which students with limited time can follow.
